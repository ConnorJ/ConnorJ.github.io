Enable Motion & Fitness on iPhone
  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Scroll down and tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Motion & Fitness
  4. Turn on Fitness Tracking
  5. Scroll down and find Safari (or your browser)
  6. Turn on the toggle next to your browser
  7. Return to this page and refresh
Enable Device Motion on Android
  1. Open Chrome (or your browser)
  2. Tap the three dots menu in the top-right
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Tap Site settings
  5. Tap Motion sensors
  6. Turn on Motion sensors
  7. Return to this page and refresh

Alternative: When prompted by this website, tap Allow for motion access.

Enable Location Services on iPhone
  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Location Services
  4. Turn on Location Services at the top
  5. Scroll down and find Safari (or your browser)
  6. Tap on your browser and select While Using App
  7. Return to this page and refresh
