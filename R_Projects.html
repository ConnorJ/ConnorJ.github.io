R Projects

In the past year, I have started taking some graduate statistics course to learn R. R is a free software environment for statistical computing and graphics (or at least that is what their website says. http://www.r-project.org/). I have been able to study with both Heike Hoffman and Di Cook both who are leaders in the field of statistical computing and arguable the best in the world. So far I have taken Stat 579, Intro to R and Stat 585x, Data Technologies for Statistical Analysis.

The projects below are some of my favorites from these classes and some personal projects.
